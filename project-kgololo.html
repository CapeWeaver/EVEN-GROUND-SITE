About this partner

Equality of opportunity begins with the classroom

Kgololo Academy is a low-fee independent school providing scholars from Alexandra Township access to world-class education within their community at accessible cost. The organization comprises inspirational teachers, parents, and community members who believe in the unlimited potential of township children.

In South Africa your demography defines your destiny. In the Alexandra Township, less than 30% of scholars who matriculate from secondary school do so with a Bachelor's Pass — the pass needed for acceptance to a university. The average Bachelor Pass rate in schools that serve scholars from the more affluent suburbs of Johannesburg is 99%.

At Kgololo Academy, the organization recognizes that true equality of opportunity begins with ensuring that ALL of their children have access to a world-class education.

The Kgololo model

The academy's educational framework — small classes, teacher coaching, parent partnership, and university-bound aspiration — designed to place all students on the path to a Bachelor's Pass and beyond.

Outcome

Closing a 70-point gap

Kgololo's children should not have a 30% chance of a Bachelor's Pass when peers across town have 99%. The school exists to close that gap — and to prove it can be closed at scale.

Even Ground's role

Funding the path to university

Township primary schooling decides life trajectories — and Kgololo's model proves children from Alexandra can match the academic outcomes of any school in Johannesburg. Our support helps Kgololo retain teachers, maintain low fees, and keep the doors open for families who cannot afford private schooling elsewhere.

  • Unrestricted — Kgololo decides where the funding goes
  • Multi-year commitments wherever possible
  • Capacity support alongside cash — peer learning, M&E, network introductions
  • Amplification through Even Ground's US donor network
