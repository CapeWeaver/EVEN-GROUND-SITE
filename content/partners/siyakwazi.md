## About — short
Siyakwazi is a community-based organisation supporting children with disabilities and barriers to learning under age 7 in rural KwaZulu-Natal communities.

## About — long
The organization advocates for the rights of children with disabilities, operating from the Siyakwazi Resource Centre established in 2018. Their work focuses on helping children reach their full potential through inclusive education and early intervention programs.

## Programs

### Inclusion Support
Home-based learning interventions and inclusive classroom support for children with disabilities.

### Therapy
Termly therapy support through occupational therapy, physiotherapy, and speech & language therapy.

### School Readiness
Red Flag Screening & Catch-up Programme in pre-Grade R to Grade 1 for early identification of children with learning barriers.

### Early Learning
Critical learning support in the first 1,000 days with parents and babies to support development in homes.

## Impact statistics
- **Half a million** children with disabilities are currently out of school in South Africa
- **651 children** under age 7 identified at risk in 2024 across 35 educational facilities
- **117,000** out-of-school children in KwaZulu-Natal
