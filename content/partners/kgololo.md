## Tagline
A world-class education in the heart of Alexandra

## About — short
Kgololo Academy is a low-fee independent school providing scholars from Alexandra Township access to world-class education within their community at accessible cost. The organization comprises inspirational teachers, parents, and community members who believe in the unlimited potential of township children.

## About — long
In South Africa your demography defines your destiny. In the Alexandra Township, less than 30% of scholars who matriculate from secondary school do so with a Bachelor's Pass — the pass needed for acceptance to a university. The average Bachelor Pass rate in schools that serve scholars from the more affluent suburbs of Johannesburg is 99%.

At Kgololo Academy, the organization recognizes that true equality of opportunity begins with ensuring that ALL of their children have access to a world-class education.
