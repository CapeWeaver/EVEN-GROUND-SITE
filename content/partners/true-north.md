## Tagline
Developing our country's most precious resource through scalable ECD communities

## About — short
True North is a non-profit organisation pioneering Early Childhood Development (ECD) initiatives within marginalised communities, focused on providing quality ECD programmes for all South African children.

## About — long
True North's involvement within Vrygrond began in 2007, initially as a research project to record challenges faced by vulnerable communities, whilst exploring the framework provided by local and national government, as well as quantifying existing capacity within the non-profit sector.

The organisation operates on the principle of "Smart with Heart" — which combines clear strategy, measurable outcomes, and knowledge sharing with compassion, understanding, and perseverance to build sustainable, vibrant ECD initiatives.

## Programs

### ECD Project
Core initiative in Vrygrond and Overcome Heights addressing systemic inequalities and providing access to quality early childhood development programmes.

### Pre-school Registration Colour Guide
Mobile app tool to support pre-school registration and assessment processes.

## Impact statistics
- **532,097** children under age 5 in Western Cape Province
- **40%** of children have no access to ECD facilities in Western Cape
- **5,000** children ages 0–5 in the Vrygrond community

## Partnership with Even Ground
Even Ground partners with True North on their work in Vrygrond and Overcome Heights.
