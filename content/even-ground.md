# Even Ground

Parent org. All partner content docs live in `partners/` and link back here.

## Tagline
Transforming the lives of children and families in South Africa

## About — short
Even Ground partners with South African community-based organizations driving high-impact education, health and nutrition, and youth development solutions. By increasing financial investments, capacity development, and knowledge sharing, the organization builds these organizations' sustainability and amplifies their impact.

## About — long
Even Ground was founded in 2003 by a passionate group of students from the US and South Africa who recognized the transformative potential within South African communities. Initially founded to help address the educational, health, and social needs of children and families affected by HIV, the organization has expanded significantly over the last two decades to focus on education, health and nutrition, and youth development.

Beginning in 2024, Even Ground is intensifying its commitment to providing increasing funding and capacity-building support to select community-based nonprofit organizations in South Africa and beyond.

## Approach — Three Pathways to Change

### Connecting Changemakers
By connecting changemakers in the US with local changemakers in South Africa, supporting the success of children they serve.

### Sparking Solutions
Investing in hyperlocal, community-driven solutions through long-term partnerships with organizations to grow programs that catalyze change.

### Changing Lives
Investing in organizations ensuring children have adequate support from infancy to young adulthood through education, health & nutrition, and youth development.
